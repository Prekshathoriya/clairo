What Clairo Does

Everything you need from a meeting

From raw transcript to structured notes in seconds, powered entirely by local NLP — no cloud, no cost.

📋

AI Meeting Summary

TF-IDF based extractive summarization distills long transcripts into concise, readable summaries.

✅

Action Item Detection

Automatically detects tasks assigned to specific speakers based on linguistic patterns and action verbs.

⚡

Key Decisions

Extracts decision statements and commitments made during the meeting for quick reference.

🎤

Speaker Insights

Visualizes participation rates by speaker so you always know who drove the conversation.

🗂

Meeting History

All analyzed meetings are saved locally in SQLite and browsable through a searchable dashboard.

📤

Export to TXT & PDF

Download beautifully formatted meeting notes as TXT or PDF with one click, ready to share.

Built with ❤️ using FastAPI + NLTK · No external APIs · 100% local
